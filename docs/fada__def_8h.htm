#define FADA_NEXT_WINDOW   -1L

Used in fada_continue to instruct libfada to advance to the next window.

Can be multiplied to advance over multiple windows. For example, fada_continue(m, FADA_NEXT_WINDOW * 2) will advance two window lengths.
